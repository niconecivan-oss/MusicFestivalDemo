About

Foyle Music Festivcal was organized with the aim of establishing connections between Irish musicians and providing people with the opportunity to enjoy music of various genres.

We guarantee a festive atmosphere and the opportunity to have a good time with friends and family.

There will be four concerts, each about 30 minutes.
